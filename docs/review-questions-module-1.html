Review Questions: Module 1

Once you have watched the lecture recordings you should be able to answer the following Threshold questions (solutions can be found at the end of this Workbook):

(i) What is a variable? What is a value of a variable? Give examples of each.

(ii) How can we tell if a variable is quantitative, categorical, ordinal or an identifier?

(iii) What is the difference between a statistic and a parameter?
What do they measure and how might they be obtained?

(iv) What is the difference between a representative sample and a convenience sample? How are they obtained and what are their relative advantages/disadvantages?

(v) What is a contingency or two‑way table? When is it used?

(vi) What is meant by a marginal distribution, a joint distribution and a conditional distribution?
